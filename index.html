About HayesPoint Strategies

At HayesPoint Strategies, we bring over 30 years of executive-level experience — including 17 years as CEO — to help C-suite leaders unlock their full potential. We use advanced personality assessments to equip CEOs and senior executives with insights that accelerate growth, enhance decision-making, increase team effectiveness and elevate their leadership impact.

Our work includes CEO coaching, strategic direction, planning, and execution support, as well as facilitating executive team building and alignment, ensuring leadership teams operate from a shared vision, trust, and a unified path forward.

HayesPoint Strategies specializes in strategic planning, organizational transformation, and executive development, working with companies ranging from startups to Fortune 500 enterprises.
